The Moon Presence

A nameless Great One, the Moon Presence weaves the Hunter’s Dream, a realm of eternal night where hunters are bound to its will. Beckoned by Laurence and his associates under the paleblood sky, it descends with the Blood Moon, its tentacles a crown of chaos. Neither flesh nor spirit, it is the puppetmaster of Gehrman, the First Hunter, guiding blades to slay its kin.

Every Great One yearns for a child lost—its umbilical cords whisper of surrogates sought through blood and dream. Some call it Flora, demon of the moon, a shadow craving dominion over Yharnam’s fate. Defy it with insight, and ascend—or fall to its embrace.
